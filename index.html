Laura Chavez Medina

Brief Overview:

I am a 26 year old graduate student, completing my last semester at George Mason. My favorite witting period is anything 18th century and early 19th century. My favorite writer is Ernest Hemingway.

I live in Loudoun County, Virginia and I am also a mom and dog mom. My son is 1 year old and we have a new puppy. She is a mini red Goldendoodle and her name is Mei Mei.

Aside from reading and writing, what I love most is to travel. I had always traveled since I was very young especially after I move to the United States from Cali, Colombia. Since the age of 5, I don't remember being scared of airplanes but instead feeling exciting. Looking out of the window and seeing city lights right before landing was the highlight of every trip.

I would travel almost every summer to Colombia, and once there, my grandfather would always take me to the nearby cities to explore new food spots, enjoy street vendor shopping, and see new landscapes and animals. I would often write throughout all of my travels. And when I turned 18, I decided I wanted to travel alone to many more places. For my high school graduation, my mother paid for me to visit Brazil during the 2014 world cup. I can't believe it's already been 10 years!
