College Education and Work Experience

My work experience mainly consists of customer service and hospitality. Although I’ve always wanted to teach, I was too busy throughout college to tutor or substitute so I took up various serving jobs at different restaurants.

Currently, I am working at a fine dining wine and steakhouse while also doing freelance Spanish interpreting. Before this, I worked as a teller at a bank, and also as a dispatcher for the Washington Dulles Airport.

When it comes to my college education, I took the general required credits for my first two years, and when I became an English major, I chose Literature as my concentration. I took various African American and Literature classes, Latino Literature Classes, Creative Writing, Literary Theory, and varying Literature classes from different time period.

When I got into graduate school, I also pursued English Literature but I got to take different classes such as poetry, Native American Literature, and British Literature.
